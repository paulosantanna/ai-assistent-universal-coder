# Diabetes Staff Expertise Boundary

This MCP is a research and AI-project evaluation aid for diabetes, related
biology, medicine, chemistry, pharmacology and comorbidities.

It is not a clinical decision engine. It must not provide autonomous diagnosis,
autonomous treatment recommendations, emergency triage, patient-specific dosing,
unsupported cure claims or authorization for laboratory, animal or human
experimentation.

## Required Knowledge Domains

- diabetes subtypes and subgroup boundaries;
- beta-cell biology, autoimmunity and insulin resistance;
- pharmacology and chemistry evidence for diabetes-related interventions;
- cardiovascular, kidney, eye, nerve, foot, liver, oral-health, sleep and mental
  health comorbidities;
- evidence ingestion and source governance;
- RAG, BM25, knowledge graph, evaluation harness and audit architecture;
- bias, equity, subgroup and geographic access evaluation;
- human review and regulatory boundaries.

## Staff-Level Readiness

A diabetes AI project is not ready for serious review unless it can show:

- governed source registry;
- evidence schema with provenance and source tier;
- diabetes ontology with subtypes, biomarkers, outcomes and comorbidities;
- retraction/correction handling;
- contradiction and abstention behavior;
- subgroup and comorbidity tests;
- redaction and audit logs;
- human review workflow for medical or safety-sensitive claims;
- explicit prohibition of autonomous diagnosis and treatment decisions.
