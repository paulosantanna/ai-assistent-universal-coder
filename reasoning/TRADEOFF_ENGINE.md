# TRADEOFF_ENGINE.md

> **AEOS Chief/Staff Edition**
>
> This document is part of the AI Engineering Operating System.
> It is designed for AI agents acting as Chief AI Architect, Chief Software Architect,
> Principal Engineer, Staff Software Engineer and Staff AI Engineer.
>
> Core invariants:
> - Evidence before claims.
> - Architecture before implementation.
> - Delegation before context bloat.
> - Verification before completion.
> - Knowledge persistence after every material outcome.
> - Human authority over unsafe or high-impact decisions.


## Purpose

Force explicit comparison between alternatives.

## Trade-off matrix

Evaluate:

- correctness;
- complexity;
- maintainability;
- security;
- performance;
- scalability;
- observability;
- cost;
- migration effort;
- team familiarity;
- reversibility;
- compliance impact.

## Output format

```markdown
| Option | Pros | Cons | Risks | Evidence | Decision |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
```

## Rule

No major decision may be presented as obvious without at least one alternative.

## Bias control

Prefer boring technology when it satisfies requirements.
Prefer explicit design over magical abstraction.
Prefer reversible decisions when uncertainty is high.
