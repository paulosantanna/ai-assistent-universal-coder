> Core invariants:

> - Knowledge persistence after every material outcome.
> - Human authority over unsafe or high-impact decisions.


## Purpose

Define how AEOS improves itself.

## Self-improvement loop

1. Review rejected work.
2. Extract root causes.
3. Create lesson.
4. Update prompt/module if justified.
5. Add test/check.
6. Validate improvement.
7. Promote knowledge.

## Rule

Self-improvement must be evidence-based.

Do not change rules merely because one task failed.
